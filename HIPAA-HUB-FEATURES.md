## Overview

HIPAA Hub is a comprehensive compliance operating system designed to help small and mid-sized healthcare practices achieve and maintain HIPAA compliance. The platform provides automated document generation, risk assessment, evidence management, employee training, and audit preparation tools.

**Key Principles:**
- Zero PHI storage (only compliance documentation)
- OCR audit-aligned processes
- Defensible evidence collection
- Automated compliance tracking
- One-click audit package export

---

## Core Modules

### 1. Security Risk Analysis (SRA)
- **150+ OCR-aligned controls** mapped to NIST SP 800-66
- **Automated risk scoring** based on assessment answers
- **Remediation roadmap** with prioritized action items
- **Annual assessment** tracking and reminders
- **Risk level classification**: Low, Medium, High, Critical
- **Comprehensive reporting** for audit defense

### 2. Policy Engine
- **9 Required HIPAA Policies** automatically generated:
  1. HIPAA Security & Privacy Master Policy (MST-001)
  2. Security Risk Analysis (SRA) Policy (POL-002)
  3. Risk Management Plan (POL-003)
  4. Access Control Policy (POL-004)
  5. Workforce Training Policy (POL-005)
  6. Sanction Policy (POL-006)
  7. Incident Response & Breach Notification (POL-007)
  8. Business Associate Management (POL-008)
  9. Audit Logs & Documentation Retention (POL-009)
- **AI-powered generation** based on organization data and risk assessment
- **Version control** with legal timestamps
- **Digital signatures** support
- **Auto-filled** with practice-specific information
- **Exportable** in PDF/DOCX formats

### 3. Evidence Vault
- **AES-256 encrypted storage** for all compliance evidence
- **Control-level evidence mapping** to specific HIPAA requirements
- **48 evidence fields** organized by HIPAA categories:
  - Administrative Safeguards
  - Physical Safeguards
  - Technical Safeguards
  - Breach Response
  - Workforce Compliance
  - Vendor Compliance
  - Audit & Logging
  - Risk Management
  - Privacy Rule
  - Security Rule
- **Immutable audit logs** for all uploads and changes
- **Automatic retention rules** (typically 7 years)
- **Multiple capture types**:
  - Document Upload (PDF, DOC, images)
  - External Link
  - Attestation
  - System Generated
  - Screenshot
- **Status tracking**: Valid, Expired, Missing, Requires Review, Archived
- **OCR-ready** evidence organization

### 4. Workforce Training
- **Mobile-first training** modules
- **Annual certification tracking** with expiration alerts
- **Audit-grade attestations** with timestamps and IP addresses
- **Unlimited staff** training records
- **Quiz-based assessment** system
- **Certificate generation** upon completion
- **Training history** export for audits
- **30-day expiration warnings** for renewals

### 5. Audit Defense Export
- **One-click ZIP export** of entire compliance package
- **Includes**:
  - All policies and documents
  - Risk assessment reports
  - Training records and certificates
  - Evidence files and metadata
  - Audit logs
  - Organization information
- **Timestamped and signed** documents
- **Ready for**: OCR audits, insurance reviews, legal discovery, breach investigations

## Breach Notification & Incident Management

### Incident Reporting
- **Create Incidents**: Document security incidents
- **Incident Types**:
  - Unauthorized access
  - Lost/stolen devices
  - Data breaches
  - System compromises
  - Other security events

### Breach Classification
- **Automated Assessment**: System determines if incident is a breach
- **Legal Status**: Breach vs. Non-breach determination
- **Affected Individuals**: Count of potentially affected patients
- **Risk Level**: Low, Medium, High risk classification

### Breach Notification Letters
- **Patient Notification Letter**: Automated generation for affected patients
- **HHS/OCR Notification Letter**: Federal notification letter
- **Media Notification Letter**: For breaches affecting 500+ individuals
- **Customization**: Organization-specific details automatically included

### Notification Timelines
- **Patient Notification**: Within 60 days of discovery
- **HHS Notification**: 
  - Within 60 days for breaches < 500 individuals
  - Within 60 days for breaches ≥ 500 individuals
- **Media Notification**: Within 60 days for breaches ≥ 500 individuals

### Incident Management
- **Status Tracking**: Under Review, Resolved, In Progress
- **Action Logs**: Documented response actions
- **Follow-up Tasks**: Reminders for required actions
- **Evidence Collection**: Attach relevant documentation
