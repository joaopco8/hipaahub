## Factors That Affect Fine Amount

### 1. Violation Severity
- **Tier 1 (Unknowing):** $100 - $50,000
- **Tier 2 (Reasonable Cause):** $1,000 - $50,000
- **Tier 3 (Willful Neglect, Corrected):** $10,000 - $50,000
- **Tier 4 (Willful Neglect, Not Corrected):** $50,000+

### 2. Number of Violations
- **Single violation:** $10,000 - $50,000
- **Multiple violations:** $50,000 - $200,000
- **Systematic violations:** $200,000 - $1.5 million

### 3. Practice Size
- **Solo practitioner:** $50,000 - $75,000
- **Small clinic (2-10 employees):** $75,000 - $150,000
- **Medium practice (11-50 employees):** $150,000 - $300,000
- **Large organization (50+ employees):** $300,000 - $1.5 million

### 4. History of Violations
- **First violation:** Lower fine
- **Repeat violations:** Higher fine
- **Pattern of violations:** Maximum fine
