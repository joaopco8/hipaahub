# HIPAA Fine Calculator: How Much Could You Owe?

**One HIPAA violation can cost your practice $10,000-$50,000. Most clinics have 5-10 violations they don't even know about.**

Here's how to calculate your risk—and how to protect yourself.

## The Reality of HIPAA Fines

The Office for Civil Rights (OCR) doesn't care if you're a small practice. They've fined solo practitioners $50,000. They've fined small clinics $100,000+. 

**The problem:** Most clinics think "we're too small to get fined." That's not true.

**The reality:** Small practices are actually more likely to get fined because they lack proper compliance documentation.

## HIPAA Fine Tiers

HIPAA fines are organized into four tiers based on severity:

### Tier 1: Unknowing Violations
- **Range:** $100 - $50,000 per violation
- **Maximum:** $1.5 million per year
- **Example:** You didn't know a policy was required, but you should have known

### Tier 2: Reasonable Cause
- **Range:** $1,000 - $50,000 per violation
- **Maximum:** $1.5 million per year
- **Example:** You knew about the requirement but didn't comply due to oversight

### Tier 3: Willful Neglect (Corrected)
- **Range:** $10,000 - $50,000 per violation
- **Maximum:** $1.5 million per year
- **Example:** You knew about the requirement, ignored it, but fixed it within 30 days

### Tier 4: Willful Neglect (Not Corrected)
- **Range:** $50,000+ per violation
- **Maximum:** $1.5 million per year
- **Example:** You knew about the requirement, ignored it, and didn't fix it

## Calculate Your Risk

**Most small clinics have 5-10 violations.** Here's how to estimate your potential fine:

1. **Count your violations:**
   - Missing policies: 1-3 violations
   - Missing risk assessment: 1 violation
   - Unencrypted devices: 1 violation per device
   - Missing BAAs: 1 violation per vendor
   - Incomplete training: 1 violation per untrained staff member

2. **Estimate your tier:**
   - Tier 1: You didn't know (rare)
   - Tier 2: You knew but didn't comply (common)
   - Tier 3: You ignored it but fixed it (less common)
   - Tier 4: You ignored it and didn't fix it (rare but severe)

3. **Calculate your fine:**
   - Tier 2 average: $10,000 per violation
   - 5 violations × $10,000 = **$50,000**
   - 10 violations × $10,000 = **$100,000**
