# HIPAA Compliance for Clinics in Oregon

**Oregon clinics must comply with federal HIPAA plus state-specific regulations. Here's what you need to know.**

HIPAA is federal law, but Oregon has additional requirements. You need to comply with both federal HIPAA and Oregon-specific regulations.

## Federal HIPAA Requirements

**All Oregon clinics must comply with:**
- HIPAA Privacy Rule
- HIPAA Security Rule
- HIPAA Breach Notification Rule
- All 9 required policies
- Risk assessment
- Staff training
- Business Associate Agreements (BAAs)

**See our [Complete HIPAA Compliance Guide](/blog/complete-hipaa-compliance-guide) for federal requirements.**

## Oregon-Specific Requirements

### 1. State Breach Notification

**Requirements:**
- Notify patients within 60 days (same as HIPAA)
- Notify Oregon Attorney General for breaches affecting 500+ residents
- Additional notification requirements

**HIPAA requirement:** 60 days
**Oregon requirement:** 60 days (same as HIPAA)

**You must comply with both requirements.**
