## What is an Audit Package?

An audit package is a complete collection of all your compliance documentation, organized and ready to present to an auditor. HIPAA Hub can generate this with one click.

## What's Included

Your audit package contains:

**Policies**
- All 9 required HIPAA policies
- Approved versions
- Version history

**Risk Assessment**
- Your Security Risk Analysis results
- Risk level and score
- Action items and remediation plans

**Training Records**
- Employee training certificates
- Completion dates
- Training modules completed

**Evidence Files**
- All uploaded evidence
- Connected to policies
- Organized by category

**Organization Information**
- Practice details
- Key personnel
- Contact information

**Audit Logs**
- System activity logs
- Document changes
- User actions

## How to Export

### Step 1: Go to Export Section

1. From your Dashboard, click "Export" or "Audit Package"
2. You'll see what will be included in your export
3. Review the checklist

### Step 2: Review What's Included

Before exporting, HIPAA Hub shows you:
- What policies are included
- What evidence is included
- What training records are included
- What might be missing

**Check for:**
- All 9 policies are approved
- Evidence is uploaded for each policy
- Training is complete for all staff
- Risk assessment is current

### Step 3: Generate the Package

1. Click "Export Audit Package"
2. HIPAA Hub will:
   - Collect all documents
   - Organize them by category
   - Create a ZIP file
   - Include a table of contents

**Time required:** 1-2 minutes

### Step 4: Download

1. The ZIP file will be ready to download
2. Click "Download"
3. Save it to a secure location
4. The file is timestamped with the export date

## When to Export

**Before an Audit**
- Export when you know an audit is coming
- Review the package yourself first
- Make sure everything is included

**Regular Exports**
- Export quarterly or annually
- Keep historical copies
- Track your progress over time

**After Major Updates**
- Export after completing major compliance work
- Document your improvements
- Show progress to stakeholders

## Best Practices

**Review Before Exporting**
- Check that all policies are approved
- Verify evidence is uploaded
- Confirm training is complete
- Review risk assessment results

**Keep Multiple Copies**
- Export regularly
- Store in multiple secure locations
- Keep historical versions

**Test the Package**
- Open the ZIP file
- Verify all files are included
- Check that files open correctly
- Review the organization

## Common Questions

**Q: How long does it take to export?**
A: Usually 1-2 minutes, depending on how many files you have.

**Q: What format is the package in?**
A: A ZIP file containing organized folders and PDF documents.

**Q: Can I customize what's included?**
A: The package includes everything by default, but you can review and exclude items if needed.

**Q: Is the package secure?**
A: Yes, it's generated securely and you should store it in a secure location.

**Q: How big is the file?**
A: Depends on how much evidence you have. Usually 5-50MB.

**Q: Can I export multiple times?**
A: Yes, you can export as many times as you need. Each export is timestamped.
